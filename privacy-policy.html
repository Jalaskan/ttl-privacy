01 — Overview

The short version

🔒 Our core commitment

Time To Learn does not sell your data. We do not display advertisements. We do not build advertising profiles. Your lesson content belongs to you and is stored privately in your iCloud account.

This Privacy Policy explains how Time To Learn ("TTL", "we", "us"), developed by Jamie Anthony Bright, collects, uses and protects your information when you use the TTL iOS application.

By using TTL you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, contact us at jamieasbright@gmail.com.

02 — Data We Collect

What we collect and why

TTL collects the minimum data necessary to deliver personalised audio lessons. Here is exactly what we collect:

Data Purpose Stored where
Lesson subjects Generating your audio lesson via Claude AI Sent to Anthropic API, stored in your iCloud
Journey details Calculating lesson duration via Apple Maps Processed locally on device, not stored
Saved lessons Your personal lesson library Your private iCloud account only
Voice preference Remembering your narrator choice Local device storage only
Daily usage Enforcing the 2-hour daily listening limit Local device storage only
Lesson content (anonymised) Building future lesson libraries (v3) Public CloudKit database, no user identifiers
Approximate location Pre-filling your journey start point Never stored or transmitted
📍 Location data

TTL requests your approximate location solely to pre-fill the "From" field in Journey mode. Your location is used on-device only and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

03 — Third Party Services

Services we use

TTL uses the following third-party services to deliver its core functionality. Your lesson subject and script content is sent to these services in order to generate your lesson.

  • Anthropic
    Claude AI
    Your lesson subject and depth preference are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate the lesson script. Anthropic processes this data under their own Privacy Policy. We do not send any personally identifiable information to Anthropic.
  • ElevenLabs
    Text to Speech
    Your generated lesson script is sent to ElevenLabs to produce the audio narration. ElevenLabs processes this under their Privacy Policy. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Unsplash
    Photography
    Your lesson subject is sent to the Unsplash API to find a relevant photograph. Unsplash processes search queries under their Privacy Policy.
  • Apple MapKit
    Journey timing
    Your journey From and To locations are passed to Apple's MapKit framework to calculate journey duration. This is processed by Apple under their Privacy Policy.
  • Apple CloudKit
    Cloud storage
    Your saved lessons are stored in Apple's CloudKit service, linked to your iCloud account. Only you can access your private lesson library. Apple processes this data under their Privacy Policy.
🚫 No advertising

TTL contains no advertising. We do not share your data with advertising networks, data brokers, or any third party for commercial purposes beyond those listed above.

04 — iCloud & CloudKit

Your lesson library

When you save a lesson, it is stored in your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service. This means:

  • Only you can access your saved lessons
  • Your lessons sync across your Apple devices automatically
  • We as developers cannot read, access or modify your saved lessons
  • Deleting the app removes the local data; your iCloud copy persists until you delete it from iCloud

TTL also maintains a public CloudKit database of anonymised lesson content — subjects, scripts and tags, with no user identifiers — for the purpose of building curated lesson libraries in future versions of the app. This data cannot be traced back to you.
