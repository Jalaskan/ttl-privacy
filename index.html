How it works

A lesson ready before
you reach the platform.

Four steps. No faff. Your lesson is ready by the time you find a seat.

⏱️
01

Pick your time

Got 5 minutes? 10? 15? Tell us how long you have and we'll fit the lesson precisely — not a second wasted.

📖
02

Choose a subject

Type anything. The Roman Empire. Quantum physics. The history of coffee. If it exists, TTL can teach it.

🎓
03

Set your depth

Quick introduction or a full masterclass? Four levels let you decide how deep you want to go — and we match it to your time automatically.

🎧
04

Hit play

Phone in pocket. Headphones on. A fresh audio lesson generated just for you, every single time.
